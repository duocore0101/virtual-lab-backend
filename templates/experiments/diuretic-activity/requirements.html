Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animals

  • Healthy adult Wistar albino rats
  • Body weight: 150–250 g
  • Either sex (usually males preferred)
  • Number of animals: 5–6 rats per group
  • Fasted for 18 hours prior to experiment (water ad libitum)

Experimental Groups

  • Group I: Control (Normal saline)
  • Group II: Standard (Furosemide 10–20 mg/kg or Hydrochlorothiazide 25 mg/kg)
  • Group III–V: Test drug (Low, Medium, High doses – e.g. 100, 200, 400 mg/kg)

Drugs and Solutions

  • Normal saline (0.9% NaCl): For hydration and as vehicle
  • Standard diuretic: Furosemide / Hydrochlorothiazide
  • Test drug: Dissolved or suspended in saline or 1% CMC

Apparatus and Instruments

  • Metabolic cages (individual type with funnel system)
  • Urine and feces separation arrangement
  • Oral feeding needle / gavage cannula
  • Measuring cylinders
  • pH strips
  • Flame photometer (for Na⁺ and K⁺ estimation)
