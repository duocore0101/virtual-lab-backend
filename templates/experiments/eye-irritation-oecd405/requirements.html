Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animals

  • Healthy young adult albino rabbits
  • Either sex
  • Body weight: 2–3 kg
  • Initial test: 1 animal
  • Confirmatory test: Up to 2 additional animals

Test Substance

  • Liquids: 0.1 mL instilled into conjunctival sac
  • Solids / Pastes: 0.1 g or ≤ 100 mg (finely ground)

Apparatus and Instruments

  • Rabbit restraining holder
  • Hand-held slit lamp / binocular loupe
  • Fluorescein strips
  • Measuring scale
  • Torch light
  • Cotton wool

Drugs

  • Topical Anaesthetic: 0.5% Proparacaine or Tetracaine hydrochloride (preservative-free)
  • Systemic Analgesics: Buprenorphine (0.01 mg/kg, SC)
  • Meloxicam (0.5 mg/kg, SC)

Animal Housing Conditions

  • Individual cages
  • Temperature: 20 ± 3°C
  • Relative humidity: 30–70%
  • 12-hour light/dark cycle
  • Standard diet and water ad libitum
