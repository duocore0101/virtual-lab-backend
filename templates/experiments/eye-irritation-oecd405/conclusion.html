Result & Conclusion

Result

The acute eye irritation and/or corrosion potential of the given test substance was evaluated in albino rabbits according to OECD Test Guideline 405.

Ocular lesions were scored using the Draize scoring system at 24, 48 and 72 hours, and observations were continued up to 21 days to assess reversibility.

Based on the calculated Mean Draize Score (24–72 h) and observed clinical signs, the substance was classified under GHS Category ______.

Conclusion

The present study demonstrates the ocular irritation potential of the test substance following conjunctival instillation in rabbits.

Based on the severity of corneal opacity, iris involvement, conjunctival redness, chemosis, discharge and reversibility of effects, the substance is classified as Category ______ according to the Globally Harmonized System (GHS).

This study is essential for hazard identification, regulatory classification, labeling requirements and ensuring safe handling of the test substance.
