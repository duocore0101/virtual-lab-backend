Procedure (Step-by-Step as per OECD 405)

  1. Pre-test Preparation (24 h before)
    • Examine both eyes for abnormalities (fluorescein if needed).
    • Discard unsuitable animals.
    • Acclimatise animals.
  2. Pain Management (Mandatory Refinement)
    • 60 min before: Buprenorphine 0.01 mg/kg SC.
    • 5 min before: 1–2 drops topical anaesthetic.
    • 8 h after: Buprenorphine + Meloxicam SC.
    • Continue analgesia until lesions resolve.
    • Untreated eye also receives anaesthetic.
  3. Application of Test Substance
    • Pull lower eyelid to form conjunctival sac.
    • Instil 0.1 mL (liquid) or 0.1 g (solid).
    • Hold lids closed for 1 second.
    • No washing for 24 h.
    • Untreated eye = control.
  4. Sequential Testing
    • Initial test: 1 animal.
    • If serious damage → Stop (Category 1).
    • If no severe effects → Test up to 2 more animals.
  5. Observation Period
    • Observe at 1h, 24h, 48h, 72h.
    • Daily up to 21 days.
    • Use fluorescein + slit lamp.
    • Record pain/distress twice daily.
    • Humane euthanasia if severe endpoints reached.
