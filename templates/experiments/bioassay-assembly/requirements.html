Requirements / Materials

1️⃣ Biological System

  • Experimental animal (rabbit, mouse, guinea pig, pigeon, frog)
  • OR
  • Isolated tissue/organ (guinea pig ileum, frog rectus abdominis)
  • OR
  • Cell culture / in vitro system

2️⃣ Drugs & Chemicals

  • Standard preparation: Known potency drug
  • Test sample: Unknown potency substance
  • Physiological salt solution (Tyrode’s / Ringer’s solution, if tissue used)

3️⃣ Apparatus

  • Organ bath with aeration system (for in vitro setup)
  • Kymograph / Physiograph / Data recording system
  • Syringes and micropipettes
  • Weighing balance
  • Temperature control system
  • Stopwatch

4️⃣ Other Requirements

  • Graph paper (for interpolation method)
  • Statistical calculation tools
  • Proper laboratory conditions
