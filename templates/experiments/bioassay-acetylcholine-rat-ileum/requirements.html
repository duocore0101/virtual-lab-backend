Requirements / Apparatus

Apparatus

  • Isolated Organ Bath (with aerator and heater to maintain 37°C ± 0.5°C)
  • Kymograph or Digital Transducer + Recording System (for recording contractions)
  • Isotonic lever or Force transducer
  • Frog/Rat holder and dissecting instruments
  • Aeration pump (oxygen or air supply)
  • Stopwatch
  • Micropipettes / Syringes for accurate dosing

Solutions

  • Tyrode's Solution (Physiological salt solution for mammalian tissue)
  • Acetylcholine stock solution (standard)
  • Unknown/Test ACh solution

Tyrode's Solution Composition (g/L)

  • NaCl – 8.0
  • KCl – 0.2
  • CaCl₂ – 0.2
  • MgSO₄ – 0.1
  • NaH₂PO₄ – 0.05
  • NaHCO₃ – 1.0
  • Glucose – 1.0

pH: Approximately 7.4
