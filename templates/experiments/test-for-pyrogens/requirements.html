Requirements

Animal

  • Three healthy adult rabbits of either sex, weighing not less than 1.5 kg.
  • House the animals individually in an area of uniform temperature (± 2°C) with uniform humidity.

Materials

  • A) All glassware, syringes and needles washed with water for injection and heated in a hot air oven at 250°C for 30 minutes.
  • B) Treat all diluents and solutions to assure that they are sterile and pyrogen free.
  • C) Rabbit holder – It should retain animal with loosely fitting neck stocks and rest of the body remains relatively free.
  • D) Room temperature should be within 30°C to that of rabbit housed area.
  • E) Preparation of sample – Dissolve the substance being tested in or dilute with pyrogen-free saline solution, and warm it to approximately 38.5°C before injection.
