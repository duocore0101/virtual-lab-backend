Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animals

  • Swiss albino mice
  • Body weight: 20–30 g
  • Either sex
  • Number of animals: 4–6 per group
  • Animals acclimatized to laboratory conditions

Drugs and Chemicals

  • Vehicle: Normal saline / distilled water
  • CNS depressant: Chlorpromazine (3–5 mg/kg, i.p.)
  • CNS stimulant: Caffeine (3–10 mg/kg, i.p.)

Apparatus and Instruments

  • Digital actophotometer
  • Activity cage with infrared / photocell sensors
  • Light source and digital counter
  • Timer and display unit
