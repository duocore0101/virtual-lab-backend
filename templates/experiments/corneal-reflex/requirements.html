Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animal

  • Rabbit (1.5–2.5 kg)
  • Healthy, active and acclimatized before experiment
  • No pre-existing ocular infection or injury

Drugs

  • Standard Drug: Lignocaine HCl 2%
  • Test Drug: Procaine HCl 2%
  • Control: Normal Saline

Equipment / Apparatus

  • Dropper
  • Sterile blunt probe or fine hair
  • Stopwatch
  • Cotton
  • Spirit for cleaning

Ethical Considerations

  • CPCSEA guidelines followed
  • Minimum animal handling ensured
  • No corneal injury caused during procedure
  • Experiment conducted under supervision
