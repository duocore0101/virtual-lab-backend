Result & Conclusion

Result

The frog’s heart was successfully isolated and mounted on the assembly setup. The isolated heart showed regular rhythmic contractions when perfused with frog Ringer solution.

Conclusion

  • Dissection of an animal for laboratory experimentation should be carried out scientifically and holistically.
  • Proper selection of cannula is essential for successful cannulation.
  • Appropriate recording device should be selected for accurate observation of cardiac activity.
  • Understanding the operational features of the kymograph is necessary for correct recording and interpretation of heart contractions.
