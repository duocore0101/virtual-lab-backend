Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animal

  • Animal: Frog

Physiological Solution

Frog Ringer’s Solution Composition (per liter)

  • NaCl: 6.5 g
  • KCl: 0.14 g
  • CaCl2: 0.12 g
  • NaHCO3: 0.2 g
  • NaH2PO4: 0.01 g
  • Glucose: 2.0 g
  • Distilled water up to 1 L
  • Solution should be aerated for proper oxygenation

Apparatus and Instruments

  • Frog board
  • Dissection kit (forceps, scissors, pithing needle)
  • Perfusion setup:
    • Mariotte bottle (reservoir)
    • Rubber tubing
    • Venous cannula (Syme’s cannula or glass cannula)
  • Iron stand with clamps
  • Recording apparatus:
    • Kymograph / rotating drum
    • Starling’s heart lever
    • Thread and pins
  • Syringe for drug injection
