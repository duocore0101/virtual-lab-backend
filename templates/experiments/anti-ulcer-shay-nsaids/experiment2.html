Anti-ulcer Activity using NSAID Induced Ulcer Model

✖

Procedure

  1. Fast rats for 24–48 hours (water allowed).
  2. Divide animals into groups: Normal, Control, Standard and Test groups.
  3. Administer test or standard drug orally.
  4. Induce ulcer using indomethacin (48 mg/kg i.p.) or aspirin.
  5. Sacrifice animals after 4–6 hours.
  6. Remove stomach carefully and open along greater curvature.
  7. Wash stomach with saline and examine mucosa.
  8. Score ulcers macroscopically.
  9. Calculate ulcer index based on lesion severity.
✖

Calculations

% Ulcer Inhibition

% Inhibition = [(UI control − UI test) / UI control] × 100
