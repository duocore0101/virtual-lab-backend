Anti-ulcer Activity of Drug using Pylorus-Ligated (SHAY) Rat Model

✖

Procedure

  1. Fast rats for 24–36 hours (water allowed).
  2. Divide rats into groups: Control, Standard, Test drug groups.
  3. Administer test or standard drug orally 30–60 minutes before surgery.
  4. Under light anesthesia make a small midline abdominal incision.
  5. Expose stomach and ligate the pylorus carefully.
  6. Suture the abdominal wall.
  7. After 4 hours sacrifice rats using anesthetic overdose.
  8. Remove stomach and collect gastric contents.
  9. Measure gastric juice volume.
  10. Determine pH, free acidity and total acidity by titration.
  11. Open stomach along greater curvature.
  12. Examine mucosa under magnification and score ulcers.
✖

Calculations

Free Acidity (mEq/L)

Free Acidity = (mL of 0.1N NaOH × 0.1 × 100) / mL gastric juice

Total Acidity (mEq/L)

Total Acidity = (Total mL of 0.1N NaOH × 0.1 × 100) / mL gastric juice

Ulcer Index

UI = (UN + US + UP) × 10⁻¹

UN = Average number of ulcers
US = Average severity score
UP = Percentage of animals with ulcers

% Ulcer Protection

% Inhibition = [(UI control − UI test) / UI control] × 100
