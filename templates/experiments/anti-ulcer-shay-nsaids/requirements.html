Requirements / Materials

Animal

  • Healthy adult Wistar albino rats (150–250 g, male/female)

Drugs

  • Test drug (e.g., Ranitidine 50 mg/kg p.o. or Omeprazole 10–20 mg/kg p.o.)
  • Standard anti-ulcer drug
  • Control vehicle (1% CMC or distilled water)
  • Indomethacin (48 mg/kg i.p., suspended in 1% Tween 80) or Aspirin (300–500 mg/kg p.o.)

Instrument / Equipments

  • Anesthetic (Ether / Thiopental sodium 30–40 mg/kg i.p.)
  • Surgical instruments: scissors, forceps, ligation thread, suture material
  • pH meter or indicator paper
  • Burette for titration (0.1 N NaOH)
  • Phenolphthalein / Topfer's reagent for acidity estimation
  • Magnifying glass or stereoscope for ulcer scoring
  • Weighing balance
  • Animal cages (fasting with water ad libitum)
