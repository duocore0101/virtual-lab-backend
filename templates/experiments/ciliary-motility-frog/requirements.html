Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animal

  • Frog (Rana tigrina or similar species)
  • Healthy adult frog of either sex
  • Note: Use of wild frogs is restricted/banned in many regions (e.g., India) as per CPCSEA guidelines.
  • Hence, this experiment is commonly performed using virtual pharmacology simulation.

Apparatus and Instruments

  • Frog board
  • Dissection instruments
  • Dissecting pins
  • Stopwatch
  • Dropper / Pasteur pipette

Drugs and Solutions

  • Frog Ringer's solution / Normal saline: Control solution
  • Acetylcholine: 0.1% solution or stock (1 µg/ml)
  • Physostigmine (Eserine): 0.1–1% solution or stock (1 µg/ml)
  • Atropine: 0.1% solution or stock (1 µg/ml)

Indicator Particles

  • Small poppy seeds
  • Cork dust
  • Carmine particles
  • Particles should be of uniform size
