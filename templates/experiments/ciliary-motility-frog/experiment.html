Procedure (In Situ Preparation)

  1. Double-pith or decapitate the frog (humane euthanasia).
  2. Pin frog supine on frog board.
  3. Expose buccal cavity and oesophagus; keep moist with Ringer’s solution.
  4. Mark a fixed distance on the oesophagus.
  5. Control: Apply saline, place poppy seeds, record transit time (3 readings).
  6. Wash with saline.
  7. Apply drugs sequentially:
    • Acetylcholine
    • Physostigmine
    • Atropine
  8. Record time after each drug application.
