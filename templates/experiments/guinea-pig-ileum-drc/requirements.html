Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animal

  • Healthy Guinea Pig (300–500 g)
  • Animal should be fasted overnight before the experiment
  • Ileum segment used for isolated tissue preparation

Physiological Solution

  • Tyrode's Solution
  • Composition: NaCl 8.0 g, KCl 0.2 g, CaCl₂ 0.2 g, MgCl₂ 0.1 g, NaHCO₃ 1.0 g, NaH₂PO₄ 0.05 g, Glucose 1.0 g in 1000 mL distilled water
  • Maintains physiological conditions for isolated ileum tissue

Drugs

  • Agonist: Acetylcholine
  • Stock solution: 1 mg/mL
  • Dilutions: 10⁻⁸ to 10⁻⁴ M
  • Antagonist: Atropine
  • Concentration range: 10⁻⁸ to 10⁻⁶ M
  • Alternative drug pair: Histamine (agonist) and Mepyramine (antagonist)

Instruments / Apparatus

  • Organ Bath (10–20 mL capacity)
  • Aerator with oxygen supply
  • Isometric or isotonic transducer
  • Student physiograph / Kymograph with recording drum
  • Frontal writing lever
  • Dissecting instruments (scissors, forceps, threads, pins)
