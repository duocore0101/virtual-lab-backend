Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animal

  • Healthy Adult Rabbit (1–2 kg)
  • Preferably fasted overnight before experiment
  • Healthy and properly acclimatized

Drug Solutions

  • Spasmogens:
    • Acetylcholine (10⁻⁴ M)
    • Barium Chloride (10⁻² M)
  • Spasmolytics:
    • Atropine (10⁻⁶ M)
    • Adrenaline (10⁻⁵ M)
    • Papaverine (10⁻⁴ M)

Physiological Solution

  • Tyrode's Solution
  • Composition (per 1 L distilled water):
    NaCl 8.0 g, KCl 0.2 g, CaCl₂ 0.2 g, MgCl₂ 0.1 g, NaHCO₃ 1.0 g, NaH₂PO₄ 0.05 g, Glucose 1.0 g
  • Aerated continuously with 95% O₂ + 5% CO₂
  • Maintained at approximately 37°C

Equipment / Apparatus

  • Organ bath (20–50 mL capacity)
  • Aerator with oxygen supply
  • Isometric or isotonic lever
  • Kymograph / Student physiograph
  • Dissecting instruments
  • Thread and hooks for mounting tissue
