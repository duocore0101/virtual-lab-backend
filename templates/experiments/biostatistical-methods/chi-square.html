Chi-square (χ²) Test

Chi-square (χ²) Test

Used for: Categorical/count data – protection in MES/PTZ, mortality in LD50, ulcer incidence, side effects.

Example: Anticonvulsant MES Model

n = 6 mice / group

Group Protected Not Protected Total
Control 0 6 6
Test drug (100 mg/kg) 5 1 6
Total 5 7 12

Result

Result (with Yates’ correction): χ² = 5.486, df = 1, p = 0.0192 (< 0.05) → Significant anticonvulsant activity.
