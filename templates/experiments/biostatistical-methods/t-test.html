Student’s t-test

Types of Student’s t-test

Type When to Use Design Common Experiments
Unpaired (Independent) Two different groups Control vs Treated Blood glucose, paw volume, writhing count
Paired (Dependent) Same animals before & after Pre vs Post Blood pressure, hot-plate latency

Example 1 – Unpaired t-test

Antidiabetic Study (n = 6)

Control: 181.5 ± 5.1
Test drug (100 mg/kg): 128.2 ± 5.4

Result: t = 7.433, df = 10, p < 0.001

Highly significant

Example 2 – Paired t-test

Hypotensive Activity

Before treatment: 122.0 ± 2.3 mmHg
After treatment: 105.0 ± 2.1 mmHg

Result: t = 39.53, df = 5, p < 0.001

Highly significant reduction
