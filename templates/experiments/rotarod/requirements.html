Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animals

  • Swiss albino mice
  • Body weight: 20–35 g
  • Either sex
  • Number of animals: 5–6 per group
  • Animals are acclimatized and fasted overnight (water ad libitum)

Drugs and Chemicals

  • Standard drug: Diazepam (4 mg/kg, i.p.)
  • Vehicle: Normal saline / distilled water
  • Test drug: (If any, as per study design)

Apparatus and Instruments

  • Rotarod apparatus with multiple lanes
  • Rotating rod (approximately 3 cm diameter, knurled surface)
  • Fixed speed (20–25 rpm) or accelerating mode
  • Digital timer / fall-off sensor

Accessories

  • Syringes and needles for intraperitoneal injection
  • Animal weighing balance
  • Cleaning materials for apparatus hygiene
