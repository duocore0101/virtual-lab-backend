Procedure (OECD 404)

  1. Shave dorsal area of rabbit (~6 cm²).
  2. Apply 0.5 g (solid) or 0.5 mL (liquid).
  3. Cover with semi-occlusive patch for 4 hours.
  4. Score erythema & oedema at 24, 48, 72 hours.
  5. Observe up to 14 days for reversibility.
