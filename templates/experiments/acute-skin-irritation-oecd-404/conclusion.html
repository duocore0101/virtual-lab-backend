Result & Conclusion

Result

The given test substance was evaluated for acute dermal irritation according to OECD Test Guideline 404.

Based on Draize scoring of erythema and oedema at 24, 48, and 72 hours, along with the calculated Primary Dermal Irritation Index (PDII), the substance did not produce significant skin reactions.

Conclusion

Under the conditions of this study, the test substance was found to be a non-irritant.

No evidence of irreversible dermal damage or corrosive reactions was observed, and the calculated PDII value falls within the non-irritant classification range.
