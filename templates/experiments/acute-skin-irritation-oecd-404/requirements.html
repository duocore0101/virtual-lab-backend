Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animals

  • Healthy young adult albino rabbits
  • Either sex
  • Body weight: 2–3 kg
  • Age: 8–12 weeks
  • Use minimum animals (initially 1, then up to 2 more if required)

Test Substance

  • 0.5 g (solid/paste) or 0.5 mL (liquid)
  • Used undiluted
  • If solid, moistened with minimal water to ensure good skin contact

Apparatus and Instruments

  • Rabbit holder / restrainer
  • Electric clipper for shaving dorsal skin
  • Gauze patches (approx. 2.5 × 2.5 cm)
  • Non-irritating adhesive tape
  • Semi-occlusive dressing
  • Weighing balance

Rabbit Holder – 3D View

Housing Conditions

  • Individual cages
  • Temperature: 20 ± 3°C
  • Relative humidity: 30–70%
  • 12-hour light/dark cycle
  • Standard laboratory diet
  • Water ad libitum
