Requirements / Materials

Apparatus

  • Frog board, dissection tools, pithing needle
  • Perfusion apparatus: Syme’s cannula, rubber tubing, Mariotte’s bottle, stand
  • Kymograph (or digital recorder) with lever and thread
  • Syringe for drug injection
  • Aerator (optional)

Physiological Solution

  • Frog Ringer’s solution (same composition as before)

Drugs

  • Adrenaline (Epinephrine) bitartrate – Typically 1:10,000 or 1:100,000 solution; doses 0.05–0.5 ml
  • Propranolol (optional, as antagonist)

Experimental Animal

  • Healthy frog
