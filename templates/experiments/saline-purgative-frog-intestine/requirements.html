Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animal

  • Frog (Preferably large and healthy)

Instruments / Apparatus

  • Frog board
  • Pithing needle
  • Dissecting tools (forceps, scissors)
  • Threads (different colors for identification)
  • Syringe with fine needle

Solutions

  • Hypotonic Solution: 0.45% NaCl (or diluted 0.9% saline)
  • Hypertonic Solution (Saline Purgative): 27% Magnesium Sulphate (MgSO₄)
  • Isotonic Solution (Frog Ringer's Solution): Composition:
    • NaCl – 0.67%
    • KCl – 0.014%
    • CaCl₂ – 0.012%
    • NaHCO₃ – 0.02%
    • Glucose – 0.1%
    (Approx. 0.65–0.7% NaCl equivalent)
