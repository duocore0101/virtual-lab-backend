Procedure

  1. Anesthetize the dog with pentobarbitone sodium intravenously.
  2. Secure the dog on the operating table in supine position.
  3. Cannulate the femoral vein for drug administration.
  4. Cannulate the carotid artery and connect to a pressure transducer.
  5. Record baseline BP and heart rate.
  6. Inject drugs intravenously with adequate washout periods.
  7. Record BP and HR after each injection.
  8. In simulation, observe real-time BP and HR graphs.
