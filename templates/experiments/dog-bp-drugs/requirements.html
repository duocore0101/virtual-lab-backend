Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animal

  • Healthy adult dog
  • Body weight: 6–10 kg
  • Used under proper anesthesia and ethical conditions

Anesthetic

  • Pentobarbitone sodium: 45 mg/kg, intravenous

Drugs (Intravenous, μg/kg)

  • Adrenaline: 1–5 μg/kg
  • Noradrenaline: 1–5 μg/kg
  • Acetylcholine: 1–10 μg/kg
  • Isoprenaline: 1–5 μg/kg
  • Optional drugs: Atropine, Propranolol, Histamine

Physiological Solutions

  • Normal saline
  • Heparinized saline

Apparatus and Instruments

  • Dog operating table and surgical instruments
  • Arterial cannula (carotid or femoral artery)
  • Venous cannula (femoral vein)
  • Mercury manometer or pressure transducer for BP recording
  • Kymograph (traditional) or computerized data acquisition system
  • Tracheal cannula for artificial ventilation
