Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animal

  • Frog (Rana species) or Toad
  • Healthy and properly acclimatized
  • Used for isolated rectus abdominis muscle preparation

Physiological Solution

  • Frog Ringer’s Solution
  • Maintains tissue viability in organ bath
  • Properly aerated before use

Drug

  • Acetylcholine Chloride
  • Stock solution: 100 μg/mL or 1 mg/mL
  • Prepared freshly before experiment

Instruments / Apparatus

  • Student Organ Bath (10–20 mL capacity)
  • Sherrington rotating drum / Simple kymograph / Digital force transducer with recorder
  • Frontal writing lever or isotonic lever (Load: ~0.5–1 g)
  • Aeration tube with oxygen / air supply
  • Dissecting instruments (Scissors, forceps, pins, threads)
  • Frog board and thermometer
  • Pipettes (0.1–1 mL), measuring cylinder
  • Kymograph paper, ink, fixing solution
