Result & Conclusion

Result

Proper dosage calculation and stock solution preparation were successfully performed using standard pharmacological formulas. Appropriate administration volume was selected based on OECD guidelines according to body weight and route of administration.

Conclusion

Proper dosage calculation, stock solution preparation and substance delivery to experimental animals via an appropriate route are indispensable parts in many scientific researches involving rodents (rats and mice).

Therefore, this work aids researchers to ensure that studies involving experimental administration of substances to animals are planned and conducted appropriately by following the illustrations given in the graphical summary.
