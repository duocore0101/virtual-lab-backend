Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animal

  • Adult Healthy Rat
  • Rat is sacrificed ethically before tissue preparation
  • Ileum segment (2–3 cm) is dissected for isolated tissue experiment
  • Peyer's patches should be avoided while cutting the ileum

Physiological Solution

  • Tyrode's Solution or Krebs Solution
  • Tissue bath is continuously aerated with 95% Oxygen and 5% Carbon dioxide
  • Maintains normal physiological conditions for the isolated rat ileum
  • Temperature maintained at 37 ± 1°C (body temperature)

Drugs

  • Acetylcholine (Agonist)
  • Stock solution: usually 1:10,000
  • Working concentration range: 10–100 μg/mL
  • Atropine Sulfate (Antagonist)
  • Stock solution: usually 1:100,000
  • Working concentration: approximately 10 μg/mL (low concentrations)
  • Used to study competitive antagonism on muscarinic receptors

Instruments / Apparatus

  • Organ Bath with aeration system
  • Isotonic or Isometric transducer to record contractions
  • Student physiograph or kymograph recording system
  • Thread, tissue holder, and frontal writing lever
  • Dissecting instruments (scissors, forceps, pins)
  • Water bath or heating system to maintain 37°C temperature
