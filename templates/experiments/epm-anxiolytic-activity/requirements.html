Requirements – Elevated Plus Maze (EPM) Experiment

Materials Required

  • Healthy mice or rats (either sex, 20–30 g for mice / 150–250 g for rats)
  • Elevated Plus Maze apparatus consisting of:
    • Two open arms
    • Two closed arms with side walls
    • Central platform
  • Standard anxiolytic drug (e.g. Diazepam)
  • Test drug solution
  • Normal saline or suitable vehicle (control)
  • Stopwatch or digital timer
  • Observation sheet or data recording table
  • Quiet, well-lit experimental room
