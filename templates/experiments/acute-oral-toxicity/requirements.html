Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animals

  • Healthy young adult female rats (Wistar or Sprague-Dawley)
  • Age: 8–12 weeks
  • Body weight: 150–300 g
  • Preferably nulliparous and non-pregnant

Test Substance

  • Drug prepared in suitable vehicle
  • Common vehicles: Water / Saline / Corn oil
  • Prepared freshly before administration

Apparatus and Instruments

  • Oral gavage needle (feeding tube)
  • Syringes (appropriate volume)
  • Digital weighing balance
  • Individual animal cages
  • Record sheets for mortality and clinical signs

Laboratory Conditions

  • Controlled temperature: 20–25°C
  • Relative humidity: 30–70%
  • 12-hour light/dark cycle
  • Standard pellet diet and water ad libitum

Other Requirements

  • IAEC / IACUC ethical approval
  • Necropsy instruments for post-mortem examination
  • Personal protective equipment (PPE)
