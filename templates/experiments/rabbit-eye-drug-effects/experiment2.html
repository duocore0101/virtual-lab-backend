Procedure

  1. Select and restrain the animal: Gently place the rabbit in a restrainer without causing stress. Ensure the head is steady and eyes are accessible.
  2. Observe baseline parameters:
    • Pupil size (~4–6 mm)
    • Direct light reflex
    • Consensual light reflex
    • Measure horizontal pupil diameter
  3. Instill the test drug:
    • Use one eye as treated, other as control
    • Instill 1–2 drops into conjunctival sac
    • Avoid touching cornea
  4. Observe at intervals: 0, 5, 10, 15, 30, 45, 60 minutes Measure pupil diameter and reflexes.
  5. Repeat with different drugs Allow recovery before next drug.
  6. Wash eye if needed Flush with normal saline at end.
