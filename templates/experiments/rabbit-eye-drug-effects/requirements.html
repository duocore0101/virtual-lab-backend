Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animal

  • Healthy rabbit (Albino New Zealand White strain)
  • Body weight: 2–3 kg

Drugs and Solutions

  • Atropine sulphate (0.1–1%) – Mydriatic
  • Pilocarpine nitrate (0.1–1%) – Miotic
  • Normal saline (Control)

Apparatus and Instruments

  • Rabbit holder / restrainer box
  • Torch light
  • Measuring scale / ruler (mm)
  • Dropper for instillation
