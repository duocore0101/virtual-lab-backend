Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animal

  • Albino rat (150–250 g)
  • Either sex
  • Overnight fasted (water ad libitum)

Drugs and Chemicals

  • Noradrenaline (NA) stock (10⁻³ M or appropriate dilution) – Agonist
  • Prazosin stock (10⁻⁴ M or 1 mg/mL) – Selective α₁ antagonist
  • Physiological Salt Solution (PSS) – Krebs solution (preferred) or Tyrode’s solution
  • pH maintained at 7.4

Apparatus and Instruments

  • Student organ bath (20–25 mL) or thermostatically controlled isolated tissue bath
  • Isometric force transducer with digital recorder
  • OR Frontal writing lever with kymograph (magnification 7–10×)
  • Aeration with carbogen (95% O₂ + 5% CO₂)
  • Temperature maintained at 37 ± 0.5°C
  • Resting tension: 0.5–1 g

Accessories

  • Dissecting instruments
  • Petri dish
  • Thread for mounting tissue
  • Aerator setup
  • Stopwatch
