Procedure

Animal: Albino mice

Temperature: 55 ± 0.5 °C

Cut-off time: 15 sec

Reaction: Paw licking / Jumping

  1. Set the hot plate to 55 ± 0.5°C.
  2. Record baseline reaction time.
  3. Divide animals into Control, Standard and Test groups.
  4. Administer drugs.
  5. Record reaction time at 30, 60, 90 and 120 min.
