Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animals

  • Healthy Swiss Albino Mice (20–30 g) or Rats
  • Animals fasted overnight
  • Screened for baseline response
  • Animals with baseline reaction time > 15 seconds are excluded

Instruments / Apparatus

  • Eddy's Hot Plate Analgesiometer (Heated metal plate with temperature controller)
  • Transparent enclosure / cylinder to confine the animal
  • Stopwatch / Timer
  • Syringe for drug administration

Drugs

  • Standard Drug: Morphine (5–10 mg/kg)
  • Test Drug: Various doses as per protocol
  • Vehicle (Control): Distilled water or normal saline
