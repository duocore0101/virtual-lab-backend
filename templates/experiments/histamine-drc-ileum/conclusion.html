Result & Conclusion

Result

The cumulative dose-response curve of histamine on isolated guinea pig ileum was found to be sigmoid in nature when plotted as log concentration versus % maximum response.

The calculated pD₂ value of histamine on guinea pig ileum is:

pD₂ = 6.7

This indicates the high potency of histamine on H₁ receptors of guinea pig ileum.

Conclusion

The experiment successfully demonstrates the quantitative relationship between agonist concentration and tissue response.

The sigmoidal nature of the curve confirms receptor-mediated graded response and follows the principles of receptor occupancy theory.

Guinea pig ileum serves as a reliable model for studying histaminergic responses and determining drug potency using pD₂ values.
