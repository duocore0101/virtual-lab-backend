Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animal

  • Guinea pig (250–400 g)
  • Either sex
  • Fasted overnight (water ad libitum)

Agonist

  • Histamine dihydrochloride
  • Stock concentration: 100 µg/mL or 10⁻³ M

Physiological Salt Solution (Tyrode’s Solution)

pH: 7.4

Composition (per litre)

  • NaCl – 8.0 g
  • KCl – 0.2 g
  • CaCl₂ – 0.2 g
  • MgCl₂ – 0.1 g
  • NaH₂PO₄ – 0.05 g
  • NaHCO₃ – 1.0 g
  • Glucose – 1.0 g

Dissolve in double-distilled water. Add CaCl₂ last and aerate properly before use.

Apparatus and Instruments

  • Student organ bath (20–25 mL) or PowerLab system
  • Isotonic transducer + digital recorder
  • OR Frontal writing lever + kymograph (5–10× magnification)
  • Aeration: Air or carbogen (95% O₂ + 5% CO₂)
  • Temperature: 37 ± 0.5 °C
  • Resting tension: 0.5–1.0 g

Accessories

  • Dissecting instruments
  • Thread
  • Petri dish
  • Stopwatch
