Requirements / Materials

Equipment

  • Semi-autoanalyser with thermal control and printer
  • Incubator / Water bath (37°C)
  • Micropipettes (10–1000 µL)
  • Test tubes / Cuvettes
  • Distilled water

Reagents and Kits

  • Commercial reagent kits (e.g., ERBA XL or equivalent ready-to-use kits)
  • Calibrators / Standards
  • Quality control sera

Biological Samples

  • Serum samples (fresh, non-haemolysed)
  • Protect bilirubin samples from light exposure
