Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animal

  • Young Adult Rat (150–250 g, either sex)
  • Preferably overnight fasted
  • Healthy and acclimatized before experiment

Drug Solutions

  • Standard 5-HT Stock: Usually 10 μg/mL (or 100 μg/mL)
  • Diluted to working concentrations (1–10 ng/mL bath concentration)
  • Test (Unknown) 5-HT Solution: Sample of unknown strength provided for assay

Physiological Solution

  • Krebs' Solution (Most commonly used)
  • Aerated with 95% O₂ + 5% CO₂
  • Maintained at 37 ± 0.5°C
  • Krebs' Composition (g/L):
    NaCl 6.9, KCl 0.35, CaCl₂ 0.28, NaHCO₃ 2.1, KH₂PO₄ 0.16, MgSO₄·7H₂O 0.29, Glucose 2.0

Equipment / Apparatus

  • Organ Bath (5–10 mL capacity)
  • Aerator with oxygen supply
  • Thermostat (37°C maintenance)
  • Isotonic transducer or spring-loaded lever (1 g tension)
  • Kymograph or Digital recorder
  • Surgical instruments
  • Thread and S-shaped hooks
