Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animals

  • Albino Mice / Rats (18–30 g)
  • Animals screened for baseline reaction time
  • Healthy and acclimatized before experiment

Instruments / Apparatus

  • Tail Flick Analgesiometer (Radiant heat source with photocell detector or manual timer)
  • Radiant heat source with adjustable intensity
  • Manual stopwatch (if photocell not available)
  • Marker to mark tail spot (2–5 cm from tip)

Drugs

  • Standard Drug: Morphine (as reference analgesic)
  • Test Drug: Various doses as per protocol
  • Vehicle (Control): Normal saline / Distilled water
