Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animals

  • Adult healthy albino rats (150–200 g)
  • Animals should be healthy and properly acclimatized
  • Maintained under standard laboratory conditions

Drugs and Chemicals

  • Standard Drug: Ketotifen / Disodium cromoglycate
  • Test Drug: Anti-allergic agent (Low and High doses)
  • Degranulating Agent: Compound 48/80 or Egg albumin
  • Normal saline
  • Toluidine blue stain
  • Distilled water

Apparatus

  • Microscope
  • Slides and cover slips
  • Syringes
  • Centrifuge
  • Test tubes
  • Dissection instruments
