Requirements / Materials

Animal

  • Healthy medium-sized frog (Rana tigrina or similar species)

Physiological Solution

  • Frog Ringer’s solution
    (composition per liter)

Drugs

  • Acetylcholine chloride: Typically 1:10,000 (0.01%) solution; doses 0.05–0.5 ml injected
  • Atropine (optional, as antagonist): 1:5000 solution

Apparatus

  • Frog board with pins
  • Dissection instruments (forceps, scissors, pithing needle)
  • Perfusion setup: Syme’s cannula (or venous cannula), rubber tubing, Mariotte’s bottle (for constant pressure perfusion), iron stand with clamps
  • Kymograph (or modern digital recording system) with lever, thread, and smoked paper
  • Tuberculin syringe / insulin syringe for drug injection
  • Aerator (optional, for oxygenated Ringer’s solution)
