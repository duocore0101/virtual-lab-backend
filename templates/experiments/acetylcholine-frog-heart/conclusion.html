Result & Conclusion

Result

Administration of acetylcholine to the isolated frog’s heart resulted in a decrease in heart rate and force of contraction.

Conclusion

  • Acetylcholine inhibits heart functioning by decreasing its rate and force of contraction.
  • Acetylcholine mimics muscarinic action on the heart.
