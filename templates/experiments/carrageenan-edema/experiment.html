Procedure

  1. Acclimate rats and mark the ankle joint for consistent measurement.
  2. Measure baseline paw volume (V₀) using plethysmometer.
  3. Divide into groups: Control, Standard, Test drug.
  4. Administer drugs orally or i.p. (1 hour before carrageenan).
  5. Inject 0.1 mL of 1% carrageenan subplantar into right hind paw.
  6. Measure paw volume (Vₜ) at 1, 2, 3, and 4 hours.
