Requirements / Materials

Animals

  • Adult Wistar albino rats (150–250 g), male/female
  • Fasted overnight
  • Typically 5–6 animals per group

Apparatus

  • Digital Plethysmometer (Water displacement method for accurate paw volume measurement)
  • Syringe (for subplantar injection)
  • Marker

Reagents / Drugs

  • 1% Carrageenan Solution (In saline, prepared fresh)
  • Standard Drug: Diclofenac (10 mg/kg) or Indomethacin (10 mg/kg)
  • Test Drug: Various doses as per protocol
  • Vehicle (Control): Saline or CMC

Other

  • Weighing Balance
  • Oral Gavage Needle
