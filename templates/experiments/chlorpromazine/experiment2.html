Hypnosis Potentiating Effect of Chlorpromazine

✖

Procedure

  1. Select three healthy mice.
  2. Inject chlorpromazine (4 mg/kg i.p.) to mouse no.1.
  3. Observe onset and duration of sleeping time by checking righting reflex.
  4. Inject phenobarbitone (45 mg/kg i.p.) to mouse no.2 and observe sleep time.
  5. Inject chlorpromazine to mouse no.3 followed by pentobarbitone after 30 minutes.
  6. Observe onset and duration of sleep in all animals.
  7. Compare results to determine hypnosis potentiating effect.
