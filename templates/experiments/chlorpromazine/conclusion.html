Result & Conclusion

Result

The experiment demonstrated the taming and hypnosis potentiating effect of chlorpromazine in laboratory mice using the pole climbing method and righting reflex method.

It was observed that:

  • Chlorpromazine blocks the conditioned avoidance response in mice.
  • However, it does not block the unconditioned escape response to electric shock.
  • When administered with barbiturates, chlorpromazine enhances the duration of sleep.

Conclusion

  • You will be able to distinguish between different psychological disturbances in human beings.
  • Selective antipsychotics are available for specific psychiatric disorders.
  • You will understand that animal models employed in pharmacology experiments do not always represent the true therapeutic efficacy of a drug in humans.
  • Therefore cognition, communication and social relationships in animals are difficult to compare directly with human conditions.
