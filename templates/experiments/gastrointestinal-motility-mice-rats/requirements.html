Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animals

  • Swiss albino mice (20–30 g) or rats (150–200 g)
  • Animals divided into 5–6 groups (n = 5–6 per group)
  • Groups include:
    • Control group
    • Standard group (e.g., Atropine for inhibition or Metoclopramide for stimulation)
    • Test drug groups at different dose levels

Drugs and Chemicals

  • Test drug (unknown or investigational compound)
  • Atropine: 1 mg/kg (used to inhibit gastrointestinal motility)
  • Metoclopramide: 10 mg/kg (used to stimulate gastrointestinal motility)
  • Charcoal meal suspension: used as a marker to measure intestinal transit

Instruments and Equipment

  • Oral gavage needle
  • Ruler or measuring scale
  • Dissecting kit
  • Dissection board
