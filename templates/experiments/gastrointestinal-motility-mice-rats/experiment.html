Experimental Procedure

  1. Fast animals overnight (water allowed).
  2. Administer drugs orally or intraperitoneally (control group receives vehicle).
  3. After 30 minutes administer charcoal meal suspension orally.
  4. Dose:
    • 0.3 mL for mice
    • 1 mL for rats
  5. After 15–30 minutes sacrifice animals by cervical dislocation.
  6. Isolate the small intestine from pylorus to caecum.
  7. Measure total intestinal length and distance travelled by charcoal front.
  8. Calculate intestinal transit using formula:

    % Transit = (Distance travelled by charcoal / Total intestinal length) × 100
