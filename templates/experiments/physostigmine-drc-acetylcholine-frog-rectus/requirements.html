Requirements

Experimental Setup (Simplified)

  • Animal: Frog (Rana species).
  • Preparation: Dissect rectus abdominis muscle and mount it in an organ bath containing aerated frog Ringer’s solution.
  • Temperature: Room temperature (approximately 20–25°C).
  • Recorder: Isotonic lever or force transducer connected to a kymograph or digital recording system to record muscle contractions.
  • Solutions:
    • Acetylcholine (ACh): Stock solution usually prepared as 1:10,000 or 100 μg/ml.
    • Physostigmine (Eserine): Stock solution usually prepared as 1:10,000 or 100 μg/ml.
