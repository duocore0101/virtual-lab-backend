Requirements – Anti-Catatonic (Anti-Cataleptic) Activity

Animals Used

  • Albino Rats (150–250 g)
  • OR Albino Mice (20–30 g)

Drugs Used

  • Haloperidol (1 mg/kg, i.p.) – to induce catalepsy
  • Standard drug (Scopolamine / Trihexyphenidyl)
  • Test drug (as per experiment)
  • Normal saline – Control

Apparatus

  • Wooden or metal horizontal bar
  • Wooden blocks (3 cm and 9 cm height)
  • Stopwatch
  • Observation cage

Experimental Design

  • Animals are divided into 3 groups (n = 6)
  • Group I: Control (Vehicle + Haloperidol)
  • Group II: Standard (Standard drug + Haloperidol)
  • Group III: Test drug + Haloperidol
