Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animals

  • Healthy adult rabbits (New Zealand White or similar)
  • Body weight: 1.8–3.0 kg (preferably 2–2.5 kg)
  • Animals fasted for 16–18 hours (water ad libitum)
  • Control group (saline-injected) may be used for comparison

Drugs and Chemicals

  • Insulin: Regular/Human insulin (40 IU/mL)
  • Dose: 1–2 IU/kg body weight (or 2–4 IU per rabbit)
  • Glucose standard solutions
  • Anticoagulant (Potassium oxalate + Sodium fluoride if required)

Apparatus and Instruments

  • Glucometer / Glucose estimation kit (GOD-POD / Folin-Wu / Orthotoluidine)
  • Syringes (1 mL insulin syringe; 2–5 mL for blood collection)
  • Needles (25–26 G)
  • Rabbit restrainer
  • Animal weighing balance
  • Marking pen for identification
  • Test tubes and pipettes
  • Cotton and spirit for sterilization
