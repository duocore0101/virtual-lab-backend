Instructions

Procedure

  1. Select healthy rabbits (2–3 animals; fast 16–18 hrs, water ad libitum).
  2. Weigh and mark the rabbit.
  3. Collect baseline blood sample from marginal ear vein.
  4. Estimate blood glucose immediately.
  5. Inject insulin subcutaneously (1–2 IU/kg).
  6. Example: For 2 kg rabbit → 2–4 IU (0.05–0.1 mL of 40 IU/mL).
  7. Return rabbit to cage (no food).
  8. Collect blood at 1, 2, 3, 4, and 5 hours.
  9. Measure glucose at each time point.
  10. If severe hypoglycaemia occurs → administer glucose rescue.
