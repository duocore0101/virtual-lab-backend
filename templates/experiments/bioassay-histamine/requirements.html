Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animal

  • Healthy adult guinea pig (300–600 g)
  • Preferably overnight fasted to empty gut
  • Healthy and properly acclimatized

Physiological Solution

  • Tyrode's Solution
  • Aerated continuously with O₂ or air
  • Maintained at pH 7.4

Drugs

  • Standard Histamine Stock: Usually 1 mg/ml (Histamine acid phosphate or dihydrochloride)
  • Standard Working Solution: 1 μg/ml or 10 μg/ml (Diluted in Tyrode's solution)
  • Test (Unknown) Histamine Solution: Diluted to produce responses in similar range

Apparatus

  • Student Organ Bath (Dale’s Bath) or isolated tissue bath (20–40 ml capacity)
  • Aerator (for oxygenation)
  • Isotonic frontal writing lever (Magnification 5–10×) or transducer
  • Sherrington rotating drum / Kymograph / Digital recorder
  • Thermostat (37 ± 1°C water bath)
  • Syringes (insulin/TB for precise dosing)
  • Thread, fixing solution

Other Materials

  • Scissors
  • Forceps
  • Petri dish
