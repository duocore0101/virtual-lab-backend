Requirements / Materials

Experimental Animal

  • Female Albino Rat (150–250 g)
  • Preferably in oestrus phase or estrogen-primed
  • Priming: Inject oestradiol benzoate (50–100 μg/kg, i.m.) 18–24 hours before experiment to sensitize uterus
  • Confirm oestrus by vaginal smear (mostly cornified epithelial cells)

Physiological Solution

  • De Jalon's Solution

Drugs

  • Standard Oxytocin: 0.1–1 IU/ml or μg/ml stock (synthetic oxytocin)
  • Test (Unknown) Oxytocin Solution: Diluted appropriately

Apparatus

  • Student organ bath (20–40 ml), aerator, thermostat (32–37°C, often 32°C for De Jalon's to minimize spontaneity)
  • Isotonic frontal writing lever (magnification 5–10×) or transducer
  • Kymograph / Sherrington drum or digital recorder
  • Syringes (precise volumes), thread, petri dish
