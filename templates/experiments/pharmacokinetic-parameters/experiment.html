Procedure

  1. Record the given data in your observation table.
  2. Plot semi-log graph (Time linear, Concentration log).
  3. Draw best-fit straight line.
  4. Extrapolate to obtain C₀.
  5. Calculate slope and Kₑₗ.
  6. Calculate remaining PK parameters.
  7. Optional: Verify using Excel trendline.
  8. Calculate AUC using trapezoidal rule.
✖

Sample Calculations

  • C₀ = 25 mg/L
  • Slope = (0.616 – 1.137)/(6–2) = –0.13025
  • Kₑₗ = –2.303 × slope = 0.300 h⁻¹
  • t₁/₂ = 0.693 / 0.300 = 2.31 h
  • Vd = 500 / 25 = 20 L
  • AUC₀–∞ = 25 / 0.300 = 83.33 mg·h/L
  • Clearance = 0.300 × 20 = 6 L/h
