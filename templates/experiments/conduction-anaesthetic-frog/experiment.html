Conduction Anaesthetic Activity by Loss of Reflex Withdrawal in Frog

✖

Procedure

  1. Take pithed/decerebrated frog ethically.
  2. Expose sciatic nerve plexus near thigh root.
  3. Check baseline withdrawal using dilute HCl (0.1–0.2%).
  4. Inject 0.2–0.5 mL test drug around sciatic plexus.
  5. Inject saline in other leg (control).
  6. Test every 2–5 min and record withdrawal time.
  7. Measure onset and duration of anaesthesia.
  8. Alternative: Sciatic-gastrocnemius electrical stimulation.
