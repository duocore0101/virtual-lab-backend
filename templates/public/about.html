ABOUT G MARS

Building The Future Of
Virtual Pharmacology Labs

G MARS Pharma Solution is a next-generation virtual pharmacology simulation platform designed to transform practical education in D.Pharm, B.Pharm and M.Pharm institutions. Our goal is to make laboratory learning intelligent, accessible and scalable.

Our Mission

We aim to bridge the gap between theoretical pharmacology education and real-world laboratory experience through advanced digital simulation technology. G MARS empowers colleges with AI-driven evaluation, automated practical journals, real-time analytics, and multi-tenant SaaS infrastructure.

Meet Our Core Team

Dr. Ganesh Yogiraj Dama

Founder – GMARS Tech Solutions

He is a distinguished academic and administrator in pharmacy education in India. He serves as Professor and Principal at Sharadchandra Pawar College of Pharmacy, Otur (Pune), Maharashtra, with over 24 years of experience in teaching and research. Specializing in pharmacognosy, he has authored/co-authored books including “Basics and Fundamentals of Nanoscience”, published numerous research papers (47+ works with 346+ citations), and guides PhD students. He also chairs the Board of Studies at SPPU Pune and is widely respected as a mentor inspiring students and faculty.

Dr. Sumit Ashok Joshi

Founder – GMARS Tech Solutions
